WHO ARE WE

J6W, Inc. is a digital goods and platforms company that enables businesses and individuals to dispense digital goods such as telco e-load and e-PINS via web or mobile App. Recently, J6W, Inc. has also created a platform that offers bills payment.

WHEN DID WE START?

Founded in 2005 by a group of seasoned industry experts specializing in mobile and web platforms, J6W is a private company headquartered in Makati and has dispensed 4.3B in terms of load value and over 150M in transactions. As of May 2019, we have over 8.9M transactions monthly

WHAT IS OUR VISION?

Our vision is to be a one stop digital shop for businesses who want to offer our wide array of products to their employees or customers from telco load, gaming e-PINS, insurance e-PINS, utility top-up or e-PINS, transport codes and more.

WHAT IS OUR MISION?

Our mission is to become a distributor and aggregator of all available digital goods in the market so that we can also pass on competitive prices to our clients.

Our Services

Distributor/Retailer Platform – allows businesses to sell all digital goods to end consumer

Platform for Business – allows businesses to dispense digital goods to its employees or customers

VAS Platform – allows businesses to run SMS-based promo to its customers thru unique access codes that can dispense digital goods
